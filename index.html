Welcome to Slot Booking

Welcome to slot booking page. You can book your slots by clicking on the check boxes associated with the slot. Slots are assigned 3 colours namely Red(already booked), Yellow(on hold) and Green(available). After choosing your slot click on the book button to confirm your slots. If you want to book the same slots for all days check the Book for whole week checkbox at the bottom and then click the book button to confirm.
